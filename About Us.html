About Spero

"Hope is a good thing, probably the best of things, and no good thing ever dies."

Dum Spiro Spero- While I breathe, I hope

"Spero" is an Italian word, that roughly translates to "I hope". The proverb: "Dum spiro Spero", is of Latin origin. Some people express their feelings quite freely. Some find it a bit difficult. Some people abstain from such an act altogether. But, there is one feeling that no one can keep away from. A feeling that a small part in everybody yearns for.

Hope

Everybody has a story to tell

With this little idea a group of three friends came together to develop a platform for those who can tell a story.

This platform is for all those stories which were once meant to be heard by people but ended up just filling up the pages of some old dusty notebook now resting in one of the store room boxes. These three are people like you trying to take a step forward, towards something they wanted to do.

Now is your turn, take a step forward with us. Share your creativity with us and we will take it to the world. Because, every story needs to be heard.
